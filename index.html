We are thrilled to welcome the arrival of Unimatic’s latest limited edition: the UT4-B. Featuring a streamlined monoblock bezel design, meticulously tested MIL-SPEC level drop resistance, and an approachable price tag, the UT4-B is made to be put to the test, and is arguably the brand’s most wearable watch to date.

This 300 piece limited edition is available today in the Hodinkee Shop.

You know that one friend that you can take with you anywhere and rest assured that they'll fit right in, no matter the circumstance? The UT4-B is the embodiment of that friend. It's got a water resistance of 300 meters. It's MIL-SPEC tough so you are free to throw caution to the wind when you've got it on your wrist. It's ostensibly a tool watch, but we can imagine it dressing up nicely for a night out in more formal dress if duty demanded it. From a casual streetwear look to the golf course or an upscale dinner, the UT4-B can easily adapt to the task at hand. All in all, this is a watch that begs to tag along on whatever adventure you set out on.
